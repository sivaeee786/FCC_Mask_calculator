FCC § 90.210 Emission Mask Calculator

Computes and plots emission masks per 47 CFR § 90.210. Supports all seven masks defined in § 90.210: A, B, C, D, F, I, J. Mask D transition uses 7.27·(fd−2.88) dB; Masks F/J use logarithmic formulas; Masks A/B/I use fixed-offset step functions.

Regulatory source

Mask D transition: 7.27·(fd − 2.88) dB for 5.625 kHz ≤ fd ≤ 12.5 kHz
Mask D far-out: min(50 + 10·log10 P, 70) dBc for |fd| > 12.5 kHz
Masks A/B/C far-out: min(43 + 10·log10 P, 70) dBc beyond 250% BW
Source: 47 CFR § 90.210 (eCFR, Title 47, Part 90, Subpart I)
